♡ highlights-summer 2023

every summer gets better and better. my year following high school was one of the weirdest times of my life. after dropping out of uottawa, i got hired at a restaurant i still work at today. i had no idea what i wanted to do with my life... never did i plan on taking a gap year. i am so thankful i had the courage to leave my previous program as soon as i understood it wasn't for me. i worked hard on getting the math credit i needed to apply for carleton's bmpd program. once i was admitted, it was time to relax and enjoy the warm weather before jumping back into academics.

in june, summer officially begins.

♡ camping trip

this summer i went camping for the first time with friends instead of family at fitzroy provincial park. i wasn't sure if i would be built for the outdoors - i still don't think i am - but I still had a lot of fun. we made hot dogs and hamburgers, roasted marshmallows. we spent a day at the beach and played catch in the water. i saw my first shooting star. it was nice to live a couple of nights disconnected from everyday life.

♡ celebrating my birthday

i normally don't do much for my birthday - but since I turned 19 this summer, i wanted to go out and have fun. first, my boyfriend and I went to izakaya shingen, a japanese restaurant where I had a drink and some onigiri. then, i was treated to some dessert at amore gelato. lastly, we went to a karaoke bar where jackson and i performed barbie girl by aqua in honour of the barbie movie being released later that month.

♡ nyc trip

i visit new york city nearly every year... it never gets old. i stayed with my friend in their apartment and they showed me a ton of cool places. we went to a rooftop patio in jersey city with a breathtaking view of the nyc skyline. we visited the nintendo store in rockefeller center. i bought a tiny plushie of volcarona, one of my favourite pokémon. we also went to a claw machine café, though i didn't win anything. i can't wait to visit next year.
